# Privacy Policy for Routine Check

**Last updated:** 2026-05-27

## Summary

Routine Check stores your skincare routine and preferences locally in your
browser. We do not transmit, sell, share, or otherwise transfer your data to
anyone, including the developer.

## What We Store

- **Routine data.** Product names, pasted ingredient lists, and the
  AM / PM / Both slot you assign to each product you add to the extension.
- **Preferences.** Optional skin preferences (for example: sensitivity,
  pigmentation interest, anti-aging interest). These are user preferences
  for tailoring on-screen analysis — not medical conditions or health records.
- **Per-page dismissals.** When you dismiss the on-page analysis banner for
  a specific Sephora product, we remember the product's SKU so the banner
  does not re-appear for that product on a later visit.

## Where Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally in your browser via Chrome's `storage.local` API.
Data never leaves your device. We do not operate any servers that receive,
process, or store your information.

## What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect: your name, email address, location, browsing history,
IP address, payment information, account credentials, or any data outside
what you explicitly enter into the extension. The extension only runs on
pages under `https://www.sephora.com/` — it cannot see or read any other
website you visit.

## Third Parties

This version of Routine Check does not use any third-party analytics,
advertising, tracking, A/B testing, error reporting, or remote configuration
services. No third-party scripts run inside the extension.

## Your Rights (CCPA, GDPR, and similar laws)

You can view, edit, or delete all stored data at any time from the extension
popup. Uninstalling the extension removes all data it has stored. Because the
data is local-only and we have no servers, there is no copy of your data for
us to "access" or "delete" on your behalf — you are in full control.

## Children

Routine Check is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not
knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are under 13,
please do not use this extension.

how we handle it, we will announce the change in the extension popup before
it takes effect.
